How this temporary inbox works

TempMailFree provides a disposable email inbox that allows you to receive emails without using your personal email address. You choose an inbox name, and incoming emails sent to that address appear automatically on this page.

The inbox runs directly in your browser and periodically checks for new messages. No account registration is required, and inboxes are created instantly.

Privacy & email retention

This service does not require personal information, passwords, or user accounts. Inbox names are stored locally in your browser to improve convenience.

Emails are stored only for a limited period of time and may be automatically deleted to protect user privacy.

What emails are supported

Temporary inboxes can receive standard email messages including verification emails. Some emails with large attachments or complex formatting may not display fully.

This service is intended for sign-ups, testing, and short-term email use.
